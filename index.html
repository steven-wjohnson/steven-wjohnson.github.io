About

Hello. My name is Steven and I have been developing native and web based software since 2015. I recently graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. My journey into software began with two years at a technical college building applications in Java and C#. I developed progressive knowledge of web frameworks like ASP.NET and Spring, as well as how to build and maintain databases with Tomcat and Microsoft Sql Server. I later went on to continue my education at the University of Wisconsin - River Falls where I built a strong foundation of knowledge about object oriented programming, algorithms and data structures, shells and bash scripting, operating systems, and software engineering practices like the software development life cycle and agile development practices. My goal is to turn my passion for software and problem solving into a career that allows me to build real world solutions to interesting business and human problems. If you have an open role and think I am the kind of developer you are looking for, I would encourage you to take a look around this site and check out some of my projects, or reach out through any of the provided means of contact.
